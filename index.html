What is a Phygital Pot?

Where Your Shelf Meets the Blockchain

Each phygital pot is a one-of-a-kind handcrafted ceramic — the kind you'd proudly display on a shelf. Every pot is permanently linked to a unique NFT on the blockchain, creating an unbreakable connection between the physical object and its digital twin.

Unlike generic digital collectibles, Indoor Jungle NFTs have a real-world object behind them. And unlike plain ceramics, our pots have a living, growing digital record of the plant care that happened while they were in use.

  • One physical ceramic ↔ one unique NFT, forever linked
  • Your care history and game level are recorded on-chain
  • NFT value grows with your tracker use and game progress
  • Trade or sell your pot (and NFT) on the open marketplace
  • Future pot designs unlocked through game progression

How to Access the Beta

Getting Into the App — Step by Step

Indoor Jungle runs on Replit, which means the sign-in screen will look a little different from a typical app. Here's exactly what to expect, so nothing surprises you.

1

Open the App Link

Tap or click the Open Beta App button on this page. This will open Indoor Jungle in your browser. If you're on a phone, Safari or Chrome both work well.

The first screen you'll see is the Replit sign-in page. This is normal — Replit is the platform that hosts Indoor Jungle.

2

Choose Sign-In Method

You'll see options to sign in. The easiest is to use Google. If you prefer, you can also create a Replit account with your email address — it's free and takes about 30 seconds.

💡 Using Google sign-in is fastest. Your data is tied to your account, so use the same method each time.
3

Replit Username Screen

If this is your first time, Replit may ask you to choose a username. Pick anything — it won't be visible to other Indoor Jungle users. This is just your Replit account name.

💡 If you already have a Replit account, you'll skip straight past this step.
4

"This app is from a third party" Notice

Replit will show you a screen saying this app is made by a third-party developer. That third-party developer is Indoor Jungle — this is completely normal and expected.

Tap Authorise to continue. Indoor Jungle only uses your Replit sign-in to identify you — nothing else is accessed from your Replit account.

5

Authorise Indoor Jungle

Replit will ask for confirmation that you want to let Indoor Jungle use your account. Tap Authorise one more time. This is standard OAuth authentication — the same process used by most modern apps.

6

You're In — Your Plant List

After signing in, you'll land on your plant collection. It'll be empty on first login — that's normal. Tap the + button to add your first plant.

You can search by common name or scientific name, or take a photo to use AI identification.

7

Plant Detail and Care Log

Tap any plant to open its detail page. From here you can log care actions, view your care history, adjust the care schedule, and see when each task is next due.

Each care log entry earns you LVS tokens — once the game economy launches in Phase 2.

Install to Home Screen

Get the App-Like Experience

Indoor Jungle is a Progressive Web App. Install it to your phone's home screen for the best experience — no App Store required.

1. Open in Safari or Chrome

2. Tap the Share button

3. Scroll to "Add to Home Screen"

4. Confirm the name and tap Add

5. The icon appears on your home screen

6. Opens like a native app — no browser bar

7. Your app is installed and ready
